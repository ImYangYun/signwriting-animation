Enhanced FSW Encoder + Past Dropout (p=0.3) trained on ~101k SignBank-Plus samples. Evaluated on 32 fixed samples comparing Normal (with past motion) vs Sign-Only (past motion zeroed).

Setting Normal PCK@0.1 Sign-Only PCK@0.1 Gap

Full dataset training maintains Normal PCK (75.9% ≈ 76.2%) while Sign-Only drops to 41.4%. The model relies more on past motion context when learning from diverse data.
